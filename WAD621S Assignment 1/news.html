ABOUT US

The 4th International Conference on Advanced Technologies for Humanity (ICATH'2022) is organized by the Moroccan School of Engineering Sciences (EMSI) in collaboration with nationals and internationals institutes (INSEA, INPT, UM5-FSR, ENSA-Kenitra, Karadeniz Technical University and Future Univeristy of Egypt). This edition will be held in Marrakech, the historical and touristic city of Morocco, from 11 to 12 November 2022, at Riad Ennakhil - Hotel & SPA. (Details on conference venue)
