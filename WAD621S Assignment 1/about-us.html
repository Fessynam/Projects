EXECUTIVE COMMITEE

Dr Catherine Ball

First Speaker

Dr Catherine Ball is a businesswoman and futurist with a focus on environmental science and technology. She is an associate professor at The Australian National University (in the Practice of Engineering within the School of Engineering) and Honorary Associate Professor at the 3AI Institute.Ball has worked with drone technology, and her team operated a drone that could fly hundreds of kilometres. They flew it off the west coast of Australia to study and track turtle habitats. During this 'voyage' they found endangered animals that had not been seen for years

Gerd Leonhard

Main Speaker

Gerd Leonhard is a German futurist, speaker and author who specializes in the debate between humanity and technology. He currently lives in Zurich, Switzerland.

Tristan Harris

Technology ethicist

Tristan Harris is an American technology ethicist. she is the executive director and co-founder of the Center for Humane Technology. Early in her career, Harris worked as a design ethicist at Google. she received her baccalaureate degree from Stanford University, where she studied the ethics of human persuasion
